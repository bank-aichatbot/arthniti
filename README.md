# ArthNiti

*A GenAI + RAG Engine for Banking Policies*

**Live:** https://bank-aichatbot.github.io/arthniti/

---

ArthNiti is an AI-powered chatbot for Indian banking professionals. Ask it anything about RBI regulations and it will answer directly from the source — telling you exactly which document, which section, and when it was last updated.

---

## What it covers

35 RBI Master Directions across 8 areas of banking operations:

**Customer & Account Operations** — KYC, Loans & Advances restrictions, Interest Rate on Advances (MCLR), Non-Fund Based Credit Facilities, Wilful Defaulters

**Credit & NPA Management** — IRACP Prudential Norms, Priority Sector Lending, MSME Lending, Microfinance, Housing Finance, Stressed Assets, Securitisation, Large Exposures, Agriculture/GCC

**Compliance & AML** — AML/CFT, Fraud Classification & Reporting, Corporate Governance, Compensation Norms

**Digital & Payments** — Digital Lending, Digital Payment Security, Prepaid Payment Instruments, Payment System Authorisation

**Treasury & Investments** — Investment Portfolio Classification, Liquidity Coverage Ratio, IRRBB, Primary Dealers

**Capital & Regulatory Reporting** — Basel III, Operational Risk Capital, Regulatory Reporting

**Customer Service & Grievance** — Customer Service in Banks, Internal Ombudsman (2023 & 2026)

**Foreign Exchange (FEMA)** — Export, Import, Remittance of Assets / LRS

All documents are sourced from [rbi.org.in](https://rbi.org.in), post-2016 only.

---

## Features

**AI Chatbot** — Ask in plain English or Hindi. Get concise answers with the exact sentence from the source document cited.

**Source attribution** — Every answer shows the document name, section, page reference, and RBI's last update date.

**Bilingual** — Full English and Hindi support. Regulatory terms (NPA, MCLR, KYC etc.) stay in English in both languages.

**Voice input** — Speak your question. Works on Chrome and Android.

**Download response** — Save any answer as a .doc file.

**Regulation Changes Tracker** — See which documents changed, when, and compare any two versions side by side with a colour-coded diff view. Download the diff as a .doc.

**Smart updates** — The knowledge base updates automatically. Documents are only re-saved when content actually changes, not just because time has passed.

---

## Data

All content is sourced from publicly available RBI Master Directions at rbi.org.in. ArthNiti indexes and makes this content searchable — it does not modify, redistribute, or claim ownership of any regulatory material.

The knowledge base refreshes on the 1st of every month. The Changes page shows a running history of what updated and when.
